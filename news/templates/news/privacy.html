Privacy Policy

Last updated: {% now "F d, Y" %}

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you:

  • Register: Username, email, name, role, and publisher (if applicable)
  • Create content: Article text, images, and metadata
  • Subscribe: Your subscription choices to publishers and journalists

We also collect technical data such as IP address, browser type, and usage patterns when you use the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Send notifications (e.g., article approvals, newsletters) when you opt in
  • Respond to support requests

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating the platform
  • Publishers and journalists you subscribe to, so they can send you notifications
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Social Login

If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive basic profile information (e.g., name, email) as permitted by those providers. We use this only for account creation and authentication.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure authentication. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@burst.app to exercise these rights.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of material changes via email or a notice on the Service.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions: support@burst.app.
